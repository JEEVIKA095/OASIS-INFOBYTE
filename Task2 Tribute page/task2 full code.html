Steve Jobs

1955 - 2011

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."

Biography

Steve Jobs was an American entrepreneur and inventor. He was the co-founder of Apple Inc. and played a major role in revolutionizing personal computers, smartphones, tablets and digital music.


Jobs introduced innovative products such as the iPhone, iPad, iPod and Macintosh. His vision and leadership made Apple one of the most valuable companies in the world.


He inspired millions of people with his creativity, determination and passion for technology.
